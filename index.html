About me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Fenglong Ma and Prof. Ting Wang at PSU Data Science Lab. Currently, I'm working on the multimodal learning and AI in security. I received my M.S. and B.S. degree from the Department of Automation Science and Engineering (Youth Class) at XJTU in 2022 and 2019. I also interned at Tencent Robotics X lab in the summer of 2019.
